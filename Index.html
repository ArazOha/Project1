Influenza in California

We are a start-up pharmaceutical company looking to start a campaign to combat influenza illness in California.
Using data resources from the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention and California Health and Human Services Open Data Portal.
Our goals is to create a flu awareness and prevention campaign for the state of California.
